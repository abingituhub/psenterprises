About Us

PS Enterprises Groups is a trusted name in brick manufacturing and farming activities based in Kanyakumari District. We have more than 15+ years of experience in producing high quality clay bricks and supplying them to construction projects.

Our bricks are widely used across Tamil Nadu and Kerala due to their durability, consistent size and reliable compression strength.

Along with construction materials, we are also passionate about farming. We operate two types of farming activities including Agro Farming and Fish Farming.

Under agro farming we cultivate Nendran Bananas and supply them to markets and wholesale buyers.

In fish farming we maintain healthy ponds and harvest fishes around four times every year, providing fresh and tasty fishes suitable for meat consumption.

Our goal is to deliver quality products, build long-term customer relationships and support both construction and agricultural sectors.
